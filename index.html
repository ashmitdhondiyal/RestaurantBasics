About Corte d’Italia

Our Story

This combination of food and lodging became the foundation of the Corte d’Italia Impruneta, now a legacy carried on by the Becucci family for over four generations. The restaurant emphasizes authentic Tuscan flavors and a commitment to quality ingredients, with a focus on sharing the warmth and tastes of Tuscany with a wider audience.

Corte d’Italia, meaning "Court of Italy" in Italian, is a restaurant known for its scenic location and cuisine, often offering Italian or Mediterranean fare. Many Corte d’Italia locations, like the one in Antipaxos, Greece, boast stunning views and are popular for lunch or dinner with a relaxed atmosphere and friendly service.
